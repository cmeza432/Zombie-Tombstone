Hello and welcome to Zombie Tombstone! This site is for horror fanatics and new horror fans. We will keep users updated on new upcoming Horror Movies and News. As well as recommend underrated classics to those who are just getting into horror. This site is simple to navigate as you see a few options above. To the right you will see a feed of Horror Movie News that will keep users updated for future movies. The options above are described below:




Home - Summary of website with newsfeed to the right hand side



Movies - This is where you can scroll through the movies and find movie names with summaries included



Can't Decide - If you want help on deciding your next movie to watch, this tab will random generate a movie for you to watch.
